What We Do

Pay/Less (pay-per-less) is an initiative by the students of IIT Bhilai to help heal a world on the brink of disaster. We understand that people love paper, the office does too. Maybe a bit too much. We are in the business of trying to reduce the usage of this precious resource, by giving tasks to machines built to handle them in the first place. Moving from hardcopies might just save our hardwoods. Cheers
-Team Pay/Less
